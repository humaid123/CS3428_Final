COMPOSING
Help will appear when you click on a field.

HINTS FOR TO

  • To whom are you sending the email?

HINTS FOR CC

  • Who else needs to get this email?
  • Did you include anyone who does not need to get this email?
  • Are all the people you are sending this email to appropriate for an Informal email?

HINTS FOR SUBJECT

  • What is the purpose of this email?
  • Give a brief description of the purpose for or content of the email.

HINTS FOR GREETING

  • What is a friendly greeting?
  • Examples: "Hello [name]", "Hi", "How are you?".

HINTS FOR BODY

  • What would you like to say in this email?

HINTS FOR CLOSURE

  • What is a polite and friendly goodbye?
  • Examples: "Take care", "Goodbye", "Thank you".
